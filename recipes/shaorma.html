Shaorma cu de toate

With all ingredients all only what you want, shaorma can be made at home, with ingredients you know where they come from. Home-made chicken Shaorma is a healthier alternative than every other you could buy from the streets.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 3-4 potatoes
  • Fresh lettuce
  • 1 onion
  • 2-3 pickles
  • 1 small cup of sour cream
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 spoons of ketchup
  • 4 medium flatabreads
  • Salt
  • Pepper
